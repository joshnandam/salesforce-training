# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer data, communication, sales, and business workflows.

CRM helps businesses:
- Manage customer relationships
- Track sales
- Improve communication
- Increase customer satisfaction

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to:
- Store customer data
- Manage sales pipeline
- Automate workflows
- Improve business productivity

---

## Salesforce Concepts

### Account
Represents an organization or company.

Example:
ABC College

### Contact
Represents a person associated with the account.

Example:
Student or Parent

### Opportunity
Represents a possible business deal or process.

Example:
Student admission process

---

## Real-World Mapping (College Admission System)

| Salesforce Concept | Real Example |
|---|---|
| Account | College |
| Contact | Student |
| Lead | Student Inquiry |
| Opportunity | Admission Process |

---

## Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

Example:
Student inquiry → Student details collected → Admission process → Student enrolled
