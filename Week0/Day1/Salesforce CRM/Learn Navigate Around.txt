Lightning Experience
What is Lightning Experience?

Lightning Experience is Salesforce’s modern and user-friendly interface designed to help sales teams work faster and close deals efficiently. It is available on both desktop and mobile through the Salesforce app.

Features of Lightning Experience
1.Fast, interactive, and visually appealing interface
2.Personalized alerts and productivity tools
3.Easy access to sales data and activities
4.Supports mobile-friendly working
5.Helps sales reps focus on important deals and tasks



App Launcher
The App Launcher helps users quickly switch between apps and items.
It allows users to:

1.Open different Salesforce apps
2.Search apps and records
3.Access connected apps like Gmail or Office 365
4.Customize and reorder apps



Navigation Bar
The Navigation Bar provides quick access to objects and records within an app.
Users can:

1.Open recent records and lists
2.Create new records quickly
3.Add frequently used tabs
4.Reorder or customize tabs

Benefits of Lightning Experience
1.Improves productivity
2.Simplifies navigation
3.Provides personalized user experience
4.Enhances collaboration and sales management
5.Makes Salesforce easier and faster to use
