Daily Dose of Salesforce CRM


Working with Sales App Features in Salesforce
Learning Objectives
After completing this unit, users can:
1.Understand common Sales app features
2.Create accounts, contacts, opportunities, and reports
3.Find records using Salesforce search

Seller Home
Seller Home is the main dashboard for sales representatives. It helps users:
1.View deals in progress
2.Track goals and performance
3.Check tasks and events
4.Monitor recent contacts and leads

Accounts and Contacts
1.Accounts represent companies or customers.
2.Contacts represent people associated with accounts.
Features:
1.Quick access to customer details
2.Duplicate matching for clean data
3.Activity tracking for better communication

Opportunity Workspace
An Opportunity represents a qualified sales deal.
Features:
1.Track sales stages
2.Update deal information quickly
3.Log calls, tasks, and emails
4.View important details in one workspace

List Views
List Views help users organize and filter records.
Benefits:
1.Create custom filtered lists
2.Search records quickly
3.View data using charts and visualizations

Opportunity Kanban
Kanban view displays opportunities by sales stage.
Features:
1.Drag-and-drop deals between stages
2.Visualize sales pipeline
3.Get alerts for important deals

Reports and Dashboards
Reports and Dashboards help analyze business data.
Benefits:
1.Create filtered reports
2.Group and summarize data
3.Build visual dashboards and charts
4.Track sales performance easily

Search for Records
The global search box helps users quickly find records.
Features:
1.Smart search suggestions
2.Search across all objects
3.Filter and sort results
4.Access recently viewed records
Conclusion :
Salesforce Sales App provides powerful tools for managing customers, opportunities, reports, and business activities efficiently. It improves productivity, organization, and sales performance.
